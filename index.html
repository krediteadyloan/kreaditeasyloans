About Us

Kredit Easy Loan is an online loan assistance platform that helps users explore loan options and understand their eligibility before applying. Our mission is to simplify the loan discovery process by providing clear information, quick guidance, and direct support through phone and WhatsApp.

We help users learn about different loan possibilities such as emergency loans, short-term loans, and small instant loans offered by various financial institutions and digital lending platforms.

Our assistance team helps with:

  • Understanding loan eligibility
  • Explaining loan terms and repayment options
  • Guidance on documentation requirements
  • Connecting users with suitable financial service providers

Kredit Easy Loan does not directly provide loans. Loan approval, interest rates, and final terms are decided by the respective lenders or financial institutions.

Our goal is to make loan information simple, transparent, and easy to understand so users can make informed financial decisions.

Basic Loan Eligibility

  • Applicant must be 21–60 years old
  • Must have a regular income source
  • Valid mobile number and ID proof
  • Indian resident with bank account
  • Eligibility may vary depending on lender policies

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card / PAN Card
  • Bank account details
  • Income proof (salary slip or bank statement)
  • Mobile number linked with Aadhaar
  • Other documents may be required by the lender
