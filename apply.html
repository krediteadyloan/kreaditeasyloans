Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our loan assistance service.

How quickly can I get loan guidance?⌄
Our team responds immediately when you call or WhatsApp. Most digital lenders also provide approval decisions within minutes after eligibility verification.
What is the maximum loan amount I can apply for?⌄
Loan amounts typically range from ₹10,000 to ₹2,00,000 depending on your eligibility, income, and the lender's policy.
Do I need to provide collateral or security?⌄
Most Online Loans are unsecured, meaning no collateral or guarantor is required. Loan approval is based on your creditworthiness and income.
How is the interest rate determined?⌄
Interest rates are set by the lender and depend on factors like your credit score, income, loan amount, and tenure selected. Typical rates range from 12%–36% per annum.
Is Kredit Easy Loan a direct lender?⌄
No. Kredit Easy Loan is a loan assistance and information platform. We help you understand your options and connect with suitable lenders. All loan decisions are made by the respective financial institutions.
Is my personal data safe?⌄
Yes. We take your privacy seriously. Information shared with us is used only for loan assistance purposes and is never sold to third parties.
